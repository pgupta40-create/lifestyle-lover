# Lifestyle Lover: Your Personal Health & Wellness Companion

A website that motivates you to pursue a healthy lifestyle through personalized nudges, integrated health tracking, and smart recommendations.

## Core Features

### Health & Activity Tracking
- Tracks Health app data: step count, sleep/time in bed, workouts, daily activity trends, and menstrual cycles
- Logs meals and stores recipes
- Integrates with iPhone Health app and Mac Fitness app for seamless data sync
- Monitors Screen Time to help balance digital wellness

### Smart Recommendations & Nudges
- Personalized nudges throughout the day to keep you motivated
- Provides personalized workout suggestions based on your activity level
- Recommends nearby places to check out based on your preferences
- Suggests outdoor activities when the weather is good
- Uses location services (Find My integration) for contextual recommendations

### Schedule & Task Management
- Helps manage your work schedule
- Tracks school assignments
- Organizes part-time job commitments
- Integrates all commitments for better daily planning

### Dashboard & Insights
- Customizable dashboard to view all daily insights, tasks, and nudges in one place
- Consolidated view of health metrics, recommendations, and schedule

## Personalization Modes

### Personal Lifestyle Mode
- Calm and neutral tone
- Focuses on self-care and individual wellness tracking
- Private and introspective experience

### Social Mode
- Fun and engaging interface
- Covers social aspects of wellness
- Community-oriented features

## Integrations
- iPhone Health app
- Mac Fitness app
- Screen Time tracking
- Find My location services
